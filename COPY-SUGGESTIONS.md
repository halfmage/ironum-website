## Executive Summary

Both homepages are now in strong shape after the localization and copy fixes. The EN version follows a clean PAS flow with specific numbers and a compelling 3-card problem section. The DE version is a genuine DACH adaptation with proper Mittelstand framing, formal "Sie" throughout, consistent DSGVO terminology, and softened stats with appropriate hedging.

Key strengths: problem section narrative, specific metrics (40%/70%/100%), real testimonials with named companies, clear CTA hierarchy, and proper locale separation.

Remaining opportunities: the EN headline could be more differentiated, there's a missed CTA placement after testimonials, and the EN meta description uses sentence fragments.

## Value Proposition Canvas

```
TARGET CUSTOMER: European mid-market companies (Mittelstand) in regulated industries
PROBLEM:         Want AI but blocked by GDPR, vendor lock-in fears, and no in-house expertise
SOLUTION:        Private AI on client infrastructure, GDPR-compliant by design
UNIQUE MECHANISM: On-premises/EU-hosted with open-source foundations (no lock-in)
KEY BENEFIT:     "Move faster with AI without compliance risk"
PROOF:           3 testimonials (smella, FH Dortmund, LearnSlice), metrics (40%, 70%, 100%)
```

**Gap:** No concrete revenue/cost-saving figures in testimonials. Adding "saved X hours/week" or "reduced costs by €X" would strengthen proof significantly.
