# Content Statistics Fact-Check

Verification of every numeric/statistical claim on the marketing pages against
independent sources. Goal: each stat either carries a citation, is corrected to
what the source actually says, or is removed. Ironum's own outcome claims
(e.g. "70% faster", "100% data sovereignty") are projections/positioning, not
third-party stats — softened to "up to"/"typically", not cited.
