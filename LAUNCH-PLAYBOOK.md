## Discovery Call Script (30 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for taking the time. I'd love to learn about what [Company] is doing and see if there's a way we can help. Mind if I ask a few questions first?"

### Discovery Questions (10 min)
1. "What prompted you to take this call? What's the AI challenge or opportunity you're thinking about?"
2. "What tools or processes are you currently using for [relevant area]?"
3. "What's the biggest bottleneck or pain point in that workflow?"
4. "Have you tried any AI solutions before? What happened?"
5. "How important is data privacy / GDPR compliance in your AI decisions?"
6. "What would success look like for you in 6 months?"

### Present Relevant Solution (8 min)
- Match their pain to one of Ironum's services
- Share a relevant case study (anonymized)
- Explain the deployment model (EU infrastructure, data sovereignty)
- Mention the 2-week POC as a low-risk starting point

### Address Concerns (5 min)
- Common objections: cost, timeline, internal buy-in, "we're not ready"
- For cost: "Our POC is designed to prove value in 2 weeks before any larger commitment"
- For readiness: "That's exactly what our AI audit is for — we assess where you are and what makes sense"
- For buy-in: "We can provide a business case document for your leadership team"

### Next Steps (5 min)
- If warm: "I'd suggest a 2-week Proof of Concept. I'll send you a one-pager with scope and timeline."
- If lukewarm: "Let me send you a case study and our EU AI Act checklist. We can reconnect in 2 weeks."
- If not ready: "No problem. I'll add you to our monthly insights email so you have us as a resource when the time is right."

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Don't pitch in connection requests** — Connect first, provide value, then pitch
2. **Don't send generic cold emails** — Personalize the first line for every email
3. **Don't post and ghost** — Reply to every comment within 2 hours; engagement drives reach
4. **Don't skip follow-ups** — 80% of deals close after the 3rd-5th touch
5. **Don't talk features** — Talk outcomes: "80% faster document review" beats "semantic search with vector embeddings"
6. **Don't ignore warm signals** — If someone views your profile, likes a post, or opens an email 3+ times, reach out immediately
7. **Don't wait for perfection** — Ship the first email sequence today, optimize based on data
8. **Don't spread too thin** — Legal first, Manufacturing second. One vertical at a time.
